Life in Focus

Making Life Contagious

A collection of moments, connections, and experiences that shape my journey

First Studio Picture ✦

I enjoy trying out new things every day. A lot of people tried talking me out of a studio picture, then I saw a studio, I went for it and it came out good. Since then, I have taken a couple of studio pictures and that is something I am not stopping soon. Just like the pose on this picture, what is there to lose?

Owerri Social Hangout Moments

Do you like what you see? I like engaging with people. This does not only help me make new friends but help me learn. As an admin of Owerri Social Hangout (OSH), you can see me addressing the group members at one of our hangouts.

TedEX Ikenegbu, Owerri

Going for Ted Talks and Tech events are some of the engagements that build my spirit. I have been to a couple of events and still have more to go. Though I wasn't in this particular picture because I was busy enjoying the event, please be patient for me to grace you with lovely pictures on my next Ted or Tech events.

RLabs Nigeria ✦

Having some wonderful people around you not only keeps one active but helps you grow. This lovely family RLabs gave me made me visit Lekki Conservation Center for the first time.

Canopy Walk | Over 72 Metres High

The Canopy walk at Lekki Conservation Center is a wonderful experience and also a heart-wrenching experience for people that are afraid of heights. At the peak of the walk, I said "Uzo, let's strike a pose".

Nike Gallery Lekki | Lovers of Art

You will definitely be wowed by the amount of work Mrs Nike Davies-Okundaye put in this gallery. A house of art. This place is really a showcase of art, passion and dedication. Nike made me strike a pose with the almighty Fela.

Landmark Beach | Family Time

I've always been a lover of nature and everything about it. Being at the beach with its windy environment, waves and natural beauty is something I wish I experience everyday. When I make this money, I will have a house by the beach.

Visiting The Motherless Babies | Nostalgic

Being at a motherless babies' home or orphanage gives me mixed feelings. Seeing these kids happy will lift your heart, but at the same time, knowing that someone either abandoned these beautiful children or chose not to care for them makes you question why bad things happen to innocent children. Be thankful for your parents.

Ezienyisinachi | Udogadi

Ezienyisinachi translates to "Good friends are from God". There's no best way to say it. People with good friends sometimes are not thankful enough for such blessings. Having people stand and support you is a blessing.

PowerHouse | IT Department DCC

Life is to be lived, but at the same time, one must always remember and give reverence to the source of life. Doing so with like-minded individuals provides an edge. This is my powerhouse, and they made it seem simple.

Checkmate | Health is Wealth

Health is wealth. The gym is a place where I find peace. Lifting weights with good music can make life stand still for a while. And then there's the pingpong table—I could stay here all day.
